## 🚀 Overview
HandBrake for Windows is a free, open-source video converter that turns video from nearly any format into modern, widely compatible files. So what is HandBrake used for? Most people reach for it to shrink large videos, change formats, and prepare footage to play smoothly on phones, tablets, TVs, and computers. As a video converter and transcoder, HandBrake supports popular codecs like H.264, H.265, and AV1, and ships with a library of presets so you can pick a target device or quality level and start converting right away. It also handles converting your own personal, unprotected discs into digital files. Whether you're compressing clips to save space or standardizing a media library, HandBrake software gives you flexible, high-quality conversion at no cost.

## ✨ Features
- **Broad format support** — convert video from almost any input format into modern outputs like MP4 and MKV.
- **Efficient compression** — use codecs such as H.264, H.265, and AV1 to shrink file sizes while keeping quality high.
- **Device presets** — start from ready-made presets tuned for phones, tablets, TVs, and general web use.
- **Disc conversion** — turn your own personal, unprotected DVDs into digital files for easy playback.
- **Batch processing** — queue multiple files and convert them in sequence without babysitting each one.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is HandBrake free?**
Yes. HandBrake is completely free and open source, so you can download and use it at no cost.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
HandBrake runs on modern 64-bit Windows, primarily Windows 10 and Windows 11. A currently supported version is recommended for the best performance.

**Is HandBrake safe to use?**
HandBrake is an open-source project whose code can be reviewed by anyone. To stay safe, download HandBrake from its official site and keep it updated to the latest version.

**Can HandBrake convert DVDs?**
HandBrake can convert your own personal, unprotected discs into digital video files. It does not remove commercial copy protection, so use it with content you're allowed to convert.

**Is my data secure?**
HandBrake runs entirely on your own computer and does not upload your videos anywhere. Your source files and converted output stay on your PC.
